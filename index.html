Overview

Bennett Machine Learning Group is a community of undergraduates aimed at promoting and fostering the growing interest around machine intelligence on campus.

Our primary efforts are described below:

Reading Group
We will meet weekly to discuss papers (and research in general) in the field of machine learning. The goal is to familiarize people with state of the art research. This is meant for students with some experience, as we will be reading cutting edge papers.
The reading group meeting times for Fall 2020 will be every Saturday 6:00-6:45 pm IST with this zoom link (password ). The schedule, together with paper links and slides, is available here.

Research Group
This group will work closely with mentors to aim for a publication at ML conferences. People that have experience will be a good fit, as we will hit the ground running.
